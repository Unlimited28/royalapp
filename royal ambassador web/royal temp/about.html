About Us

The Royal Ambassadors of Nigeria is a faith-based boys’ organization under the Nigerian Baptist Convention. Our purpose is to raise boys who are spiritually sound, morally upright, and ready to serve God and humanity.

Through mentoring, leadership development, and Christian education, we equip boys to grow into responsible men of integrity and purpose.

Our Mission

To train boys to become Christlike ambassadors, leaders, and responsible citizens through biblical instruction, discipline, and service.

Our Vision

To be a transformative youth movement that shapes boys into future leaders who serve God, the church, and society with excellence.
